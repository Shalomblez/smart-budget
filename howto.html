Smart Budget - How to Use

Dashboard | Expenses | How to Use

Steps

  1. Open Dashboard and set your Month, Year, Salary, and Saving Goal.
  2. Go to Expenses tab to add your income/expenses with date, category, description, and type.
  3. All records are stored in Google Sheets (Records & Settings).
  4. Dashboard charts update automatically based on the data from Google Sheets.
  5. Spendable amount and remaining balance are calculated automatically.
  6. You can view monthly and category-wise charts on the Dashboard.
